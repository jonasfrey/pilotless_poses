# Sample test images

A small set of freely-licensed photos for testing pose detection. All are in the
**public domain** or **CC0** and safe to redistribute with this repository.

| File | Content | License | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| `single_standing.jpg` | Single full-body standing person | Public domain (U.S. Navy) | [Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:121108-O-CO457-018_(8200232834).jpg) |
| `street_multi_person.jpg` | Several people on a shopping street | CC0 (Fons Heijnsbroek) | [Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:2024_-_free_download_photo_of_Utrecht_old_city;_a_family_and_other_city_people_are_shopping_on_a_grey_saterday_in_the_shop_street_Steenweg_-_full_of_shop_windows._Street_photography;_high_resolution_photo_Fons_Heijnsbroek,_Netherlands.tif) |
| `group_outdoor.jpg` | Group of people outdoors | Public domain (U.S. military) | [Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:230430-O-DO492-970_-_Red_Sea.jpg) |
| `group_indoor.jpg` | Group photo indoors | CC0 | [Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:02_Fotograf%C3%ADa_DOS_Inaguraci%C3%B3n_grupal.jpg) |

The set covers a single full-body subject plus multi-person scenes so you can
exercise both single- and multi-person keypoint detection.
